More About Us.

Hello! We are a team of four members - Do Hoang Viet, Luong Anh Tuan, Nguyen Tien Trung, and Ha Duc Hung - currently third-year Computer Science students at the University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam National University.

Understanding the challenges faced by individuals with low vision in perceiving and interacting with the world around them, we have developed a project called Clearify with the goal of improving their quality of life.
